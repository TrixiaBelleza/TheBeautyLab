Joyce R. Belleza

CEO of The Beauty Lab

Let the beauty we love be the beauty we do. The owner of The Beauty Lab is Joyce R. Belleza. She completed her junior high school in Muntinlupa Science High School as their batch salutatorian. Ever since she was young, she already had the passion of trying out different cosmetics. Throughout the years, she begun studying about cosmetics in order to create The Beauty Lab which consists of products from different drugstore and high-end brands that Joyce would highly recommend for their high-class quality. She ensures that every product in The Beauty Lab is worth trying and using. She believes that with the right make-up, every one can be an artist.
